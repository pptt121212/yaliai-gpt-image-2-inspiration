## Template-Oriented Use Cases

The Skill can match explicit requests to Yali generation templates, including:

- Product hero images
- Website banners and advertising posters
- UI mockups and wireframes
- Infographics and relationship diagrams
- Technical diagrams and system flows
- Logo concepts
- WeChat covers
- Xiaohongshu covers
- Video covers
- Film and ad storyboards
- Story illustrations
- Concept art
- Sketch-to-render workflows
